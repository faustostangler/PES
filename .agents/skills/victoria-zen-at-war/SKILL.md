# Zen at War (Brian Victoria) — Agent Skill

This skill provides a structured, high-density analytical decomposition of Brian Victoria's seminal work *Zen at War*. It explores the historical and doctrinal integration of Japanese Buddhism—specifically Zen—with state militarism, nationalism, and imperial expansion from the Meiji Restoration (1868) through World War II, as well as the complex post-war sectarian and scholarly responses.

---

This skill is structured as a modular repository of domain knowledge. When responding to queries regarding the history, ethics, and political co-optation of Zen and Japanese Buddhism, reference the relevant chapters, patterns, or heuristics:
