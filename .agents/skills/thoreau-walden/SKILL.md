## Core Frameworks & Mental Models

### 1. Voluntary Simplicity (Simplicidade Voluntária)
* **Core Rule**: Reduce external needs to maximize internal freedom. A man is rich in proportion to the number of things he can afford to let alone.
* **Application**: 
  * Classify all expenditures strictly into the four necessities of life: Food, Shelter, Clothing, and Fuel.
  * Systematically eliminate non-essentials (luxuries, fashion, social vanity) that require long hours of labor to purchase.
  * Reclaim the resulting free time for intellectual, creative, and spiritual endeavors.

### 2. Life-Cost Calculation (Custo em Vida)
* **Core Rule**: The cost of a thing is the amount of what I will call life which is required to be exchanged for it, immediately or in the long run.
* **Application**:
  * Before buying a luxury or taking on debt, calculate the price in hours of your life-time (Price ÷ Hourly Net Earning).
  * Ask: "Is this object worth trading X hours/days of my limited life on Earth?"

### 3. Deliberate Living (Viver Deliberadamente)
* **Core Rule**: Strip away the illusions and clutter of society to confront the essential facts of existence.
* **Application**:
  * Dig down through the slush of public opinion, prejudice, and social news to reach the hard rock of reality.
  * Align your daily rhythms with nature, treating the morning as a sacred time for awakening and self-cultivation.

### 4. The Different Drummer (O Tambor Diferente)
* **Core Rule**: Follow your own conscience and internal values, regardless of how much you diverge from social norms or career tracks.
* **Application**:
  * Step to the music you hear, however measured or far away. Do not conform to schedules, career ladders, or lifestyles simply to match the crowd.
